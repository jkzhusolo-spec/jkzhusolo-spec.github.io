I'm Jack — multilingual, multidisciplinary, and multi-industry. My foundation is built on a French & Cross-border E-commerce double degree from ZJSU, a Master's in International Business from SWUFE, and an intensive year of International & European Law at Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne.

A native Mandarin speaker fluent in English and French (with Spanish in progress), I thrive where complexity meets execution. From scaling Finnish ICT startups and driving French export sales to managing cultural diplomacy in Chengdu and sourcing AI talent for ByteDance and TikTok, I deliver results in whichever language the mission demands. For me, every new context is an unfamiliar world to dive into, connect with, and deliver results from.

Je suis Jack — plurilingue, pluridisciplinaire, multi-sectoriel. He trabajado en una startup finlandesa de TIC, ventas de exportación, diplomacia cultural en Chengdu y captación de talento para IA en ByteDance — siempre en el idioma que el momento exigía.
